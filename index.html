PROCESS

Up and running in 4 steps

From download to live trading in under 10 minutes — no coding required.

1

Download the EA

Download WolfFX_Scalper_V5_Pro.mq5 and copy it to your MT5 Experts folder. Compile with F7 — zero errors.

2

Configure Telegram

Create a bot via @BotFather, add it to your signal group as admin, paste your token and Chat ID into the EA inputs.

3

Attach to Chart

Drag the EA onto any chart — EURUSD, XAUUSD or BTCUSD. It auto-detects the instrument and configures all parameters.

4

Let It Trade

The EA monitors markets 24/7, fires signals to Telegram, draws levels on chart, and manages every trade automatically.
